Teaching Philosophy

Most students don't come to love QuickBooks — they come because it's standing between them and running their business with confidence. That's the gap I care about closing. I break every concept into the smallest useful step and stay with it until it actually clicks — not just the software, but why it matters for the bottom line. If someone leaves a session still afraid to open their own books, I haven't done my job.
